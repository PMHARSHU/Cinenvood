About CINE-MOOD

CINE-MOOD ek premium movie discovery platform hai jo tumhare mood ko samajhta hai aur uske hisaab se perfect film suggest karta hai.

Our Mission

Hum believe karte hain ki har mood ka ek perfect movie experience hota hai. Chahe tum raat ko akele baithe ho ya doston ke saath weekend plan kar rahe ho — CINE-MOOD tumhare liye sahi film dhundta hai.

Technology

Hum TMDB (The Movie Database) ka real-time data use karte hain jo duniya bhar ke crores movies aur shows ka data rakhta hai. Hamare AI-powered vibe engine se tumhe sirf ek movie nahi milti — milta hai ek experience.

Design Philosophy

Cinematic dark theme, smooth glassmorphism effects, aur mobile-first design — har cheez is liye banaya gaya hai ki tumhara experience Netflix se bhi better lage.
